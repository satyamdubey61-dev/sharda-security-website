Who We Are

Building Safe Environments & Empowering Industries in Goa

Sharda Industrial Security & Manpower Services (SISMS) has grown to become a leading benchmark of reliability, corporate trust, and statutory compliance in the security and staffing sector.

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the security landscape, we operate under a strict code of ethics. Our team specializes in custom-designed security architectures for industrial parks, production complexes, premium hotels, residential blocks, and educational institutes. By combining high-caliber physical guarding with modern reporting tech, we ensure your assets are protected around the clock.

Beyond security protection, we recognize that businesses need a reliable workforce. We supply thoroughly screened, vetted, and trained manpower across Goa—ranging from housekeeping professionals to logistics handlers, skilled technicians, and office assistants—allowing companies to scale their productivity smoothly.

Our Recruitment & Training Rigor

We believe a security guard is only as good as their training. We maintain strict physical and educational criteria for all recruitments.

Physical Criteria

Mandatory height of 5'6" minimum for male guards, excellent vision, physical fitness certificate, and athletic endurance standard.

Educational Standard

Minimum Matriculation (10th pass) for all guards, with supervisor roles requiring high school graduation or university degrees, plus literacy in Hindi, English, and Konkani.

Emergency Training

Extensive training in structural firefighting, evacuation drills, first aid administration, handling crisis communication, and rapid panic-trigger handling.

Soft Skills

Training in corporate etiquette, politeness, gate control protocols, handling difficult visitors, key ledger entries, and clear communication.

Build a Stable & Dignified Career With Us

At Sharda Industrial Security & Manpower Services, our workforce is our pride. We treat every guard, housekeeper, and logistics worker as a valued family member, ensuring high-quality work environments and competitive compensation. Here is why you should build your career with us:

Competitive Wages

On-time salary payments directly into your bank account, with full compliance under Goan minimum-wage structures.

ESIC & PF Benefits

100% registration under Employees' Provident Fund (EPF) and Employee State Insurance (ESIC) for medical care and savings.

Free Uniforms

High-quality professional uniforms, lanyards, caps, and safety boots provided at zero cost to our guarding units.

Career Progression

Clear, structured promotions from security guard to senior guard, patrol supervisor, and administrative manager.

Legal Verification

Goa PSARA Licenced Security Agency

Sharda Industrial Security & Manpower Services holds an active and official Private Security Agency license issued by the Controlling Authority under the Government of Goa.

Our agency operates in absolute alignment with the Private Security Agencies (Regulation) Act (PSARA). Under this legislative compliance, our operational systems, recruitments, uniform standards, training syllabi, and physical criteria are thoroughly verified by statutory inspectors. This guarantees that your business is shielded from any liability or compliance violations associated with hiring unlicensed protection.
